Payment is required to be settled as agreed during the consultation process. Typically, we initiate the drafting process after details are collected. Payment details and methods will be provided via our official communication channels (WhatsApp/Email).

3. Revisions Policy (Strict)

We want you to be perfectly happy with your document. To ensure efficiency:

  • Window: The revision window begins only after you receive the first draft of your CV.
  • Durations:

    • Professional CV: 48-hour window (3 Revisions).
    • Premium CV: 48-hour window (5 Revisions).
  • Scope: Revisions cover factual corrections, formatting tweaks, and minor wording adjustments.
  • Late Revisions: Requests made after the window has closed will not be included.

4. Delivery

Delivery timelines are estimated based on the package selected:

Timeline starts once we have received all necessary information from you.

Due to the digital and custom nature of our work, we do not offer refunds once the final draft has been delivered. However, we are committed to working with you within the Revision Policy scope to ensure satisfaction.

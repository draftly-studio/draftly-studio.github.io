Last updated: January 2026

1. Information We Collect

To provide our services, we collect user information such as:

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely for the purpose of:

Your documents are stored locally on our secure systems during the drafting process. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.
